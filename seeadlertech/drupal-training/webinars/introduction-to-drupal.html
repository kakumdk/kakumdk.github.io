Summary

The Introduction to Drupal comprehensive webinar is a beginner-level learning that is designed to help you learn the basics of this powerful content management system.

Who is this webinar for?

This class is for anyone who uses a Drupal website. Whether you're a developer, business owner, or marketing professional, this online course will teach you the fundamentals of editing content and managing your Drupal website. No previous experience with Drupal or web development is required.

What does this webinar cover?

  • Install Drupal on your web server.
  • Add, edit and manage content on your Drupal website.
  • Manage user accounts and permissions.
  • Create and place blocks to customize look and feel of your site.
  • Download, install and upgrade contributed modules to add more functionality to your website.
  • Keep your Drupal site secure by applying updates to Drupal core and downloaded modules.
