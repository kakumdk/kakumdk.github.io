Raw Mango Chutney - Andhra Style

Raw mango chutney is a flavorful condiment made with raw mangoes, coriander leaves, onions and some spices. Raw Mango Chutney is a must-try recipe when raw mangoes are in season. Raw mangoes are packed with minerals and vitamins and are good to beat the summer heat.

This raw mango chutney aids digestion and enhances the taste, aroma, and nutrition of any dish. We usually have this raw mango chutney with rice & sometimes with ragi mudde.
