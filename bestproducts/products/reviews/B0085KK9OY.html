Comprehensive Review

The KENT Gold Gravity Water Purifier (11014) is engineered for users seeking an efficient and reliable way to purify drinking water without the need for electricity. With a focus on convenience and safety, this water purifier employs advanced UF (Ultrafiltration) technology to ensure clean, safe water for families.

Key Features:

  • UF Technology: The purifier utilizes ultrafiltration technology, effectively removing bacteria, cysts and other impurities from water without the use of chemicals.
  • Non-Electric Design: Operating without electricity makes it suitable for areas with unreliable power supply, ensuring continuous access to purified water.
  • 20L Storage Capacity: The large tank allows for ample storage, making it ideal for households with high water consumption.
  • Countertop Installation: Its compact design makes it easy to install on any kitchen countertop without requiring complex setups.
  • Sturdy Build: The purifier is constructed with durable materials, ensuring long-lasting performance and reliability.

Pros:

  • Chemical-Free Purification: Users appreciate the assurance that no harmful chemicals are used in the purification process.
  • Great Storage Capacity: The 20L tank means fewer refills, which is convenient for families.
  • Eco-Friendly: The non-electric nature reduces environmental impact and saves on energy costs.
  • Easy Maintenance: Users find cleaning and maintaining the purifier to be hassle-free.

Cons:

  • Flow Rate: Some users may find the flow rate slower compared to electric purifiers.
  • No TDS Removal: The purifier does not reduce Total Dissolved Solids (TDS), which might be a concern for those in areas with hard water.

In summary, the KENT Gold Gravity Water Purifier (11014) is a practical and efficient solution for households looking to ensure safe drinking water without relying on electricity. With its effective UF technology, large storage capacity and user-friendly design, it stands out as a reliable choice for families seeking a chemical-free water purification solution.
