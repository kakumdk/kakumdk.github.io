Comprehensive Review:

Key Features:

  • 2-In-1 Operation: Versatile design allows you to use the fan as both a pedestal fan and a table fan, providing flexibility based on your needs and space.
  • 1350 RPM High-Speed Motor: The fan’s motor operates at 1350 RPM (Revolutions Per Minute), ensuring powerful airflow to cool small to medium-sized rooms effectively.
  • Customisable Tilt and Oscillation Control: Allows you to adjust the tilt and oscillation of the fan, ensuring the airflow can be directed exactly where you need it most.
  • 40 cm (400 mm) Sweep Size: The fan has a 40 cm diameter, providing sufficient air circulation for effective cooling.
  • Red and Black Stylish Design: A modern and eye-catching color combination of red and black, designed to add a touch of style to any room.
  • Easy Assembly and Portability: Lightweight and easy to assemble, the fan can be quickly set up or moved around the house.
  • Adjustable Height: You can easily adjust the height of the fan when used as a pedestal fan to suit your specific needs.

Pros:

  • Versatile 2-in-1 Design: The ability to switch between a pedestal fan and a table fan makes it a highly flexible option for various spaces and uses.
  • High-Speed Motor: The 1350 RPM motor ensures powerful and effective cooling, even in relatively warm environments.
  • Quiet Operation: Several users have noted the fan’s quiet performance, making it a great option for bedrooms, offices, or living rooms where noise reduction is a priority.
  • Customizable Tilt and Oscillation: The adjustable tilt and oscillation allow for more control over airflow direction, improving the overall cooling experience.
  • Lightweight and Portable: The fan is easy to move from room to room, thanks to its lightweight construction.
  • Stylish and Modern Look: The red and black design stands out and adds a modern aesthetic to any room or office setting.
  • Affordable: Offers good value for money, especially considering its dual functionality and high-speed performance.

Cons:

  • Lacks Remote Control: Some users would have appreciated a remote control for more convenient operation, especially when using it as a pedestal fan.
  • Wobbling at High Speeds: While the fan is lightweight and portable, a few users have reported minor wobbling when used at higher speeds, especially in pedestal mode.
  • No Timer Function: The absence of a timer could be a drawback for those who want automatic shut-off after a certain period.
  • Basic Instruction Manual: Some customers felt that the instruction manual was too basic and lacked clear details on assembly, although the process is straightforward for most users.
  • Not Ideal for Large Rooms: While the fan performs well in small to medium-sized rooms, it may not be as effective in larger spaces where a more powerful fan might be required.

Verdict:

The V-Guard Esfera STS Plus Pedestal Cum Table Fan is an excellent choice for those looking for versatility and functionality at an affordable price. Its 2-in-1 operation allows it to be used as both a pedestal and table fan, offering great flexibility for different room sizes and needs. The 1350 RPM motor provides strong airflow while maintaining a relatively quiet operation, making it ideal for bedrooms, living rooms, or offices.

Despite minor issues like the lack of a remote control and potential wobbling at high speeds, the fan’s lightweight design, modern aesthetic and customizable tilt and oscillation controls make it a convenient and effective cooling solution for smaller spaces.

Ideal For: Users looking for a stylish, versatile and efficient fan that can be used in various room settings, particularly for small to medium-sized rooms where quiet, effective cooling is essential.
