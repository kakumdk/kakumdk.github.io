The Prestige Omega Deluxe Granite Flat Dosa Tawa is designed for cooking enthusiasts who appreciate quality and performance. This tawa is perfect for making dosas, pancakes, and other flatbreads, offering a non-stick experience that enhances your cooking efficiency.

28 cm Diameter: The generous size is ideal for making large dosas or multiple smaller ones, catering to family meals.

Non-Stick Surface: The granite non-stick coating ensures that food releases easily, reducing the need for excess oil and making cleanup quick and hassle-free.

Induction Base: Suitable for use on induction cooktops, providing versatility for different cooking setups.

PFOA Free: The tawa is made without harmful chemicals, making it a safer choice for your family.

Metal Spoon Friendly: Designed to withstand metal utensils, allowing you to use your favorite spatulas without damaging the surface.

Dishwasher Safe: For added convenience, this tawa can be safely cleaned in the dishwasher.

Pros
