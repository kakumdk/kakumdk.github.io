Pros:

Cons:

Verdict:

The GK Hair Global Keratin Balancing Shampoo and Conditioner is an excellent choice for people with oily hair who are looking to manage scalp oil production while keeping their hair soft and healthy. Ideal For: Those with oily hair who want a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that can balance scalp oil without stripping moisture. Also great for individuals with sensitive scalps or color-treated hair looking for a premium, restorative product.
