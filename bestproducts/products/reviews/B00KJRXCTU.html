Pros:

  • Effective Oil Control: The formula is designed specifically for oily hair, helping to manage oil production while keeping the scalp fresh and clean for longer.

Cons:

Verdict:

The GK Hair Global Keratin Balancing Shampoo and Conditioner is an excellent choice for people with oily hair who are looking to manage scalp oil production while keeping their hair soft and healthy. While the product is on the pricier side, users generally feel that the quality justifies the cost, especially for those looking for a gentle, effective solution for oily hair. Ideal For: Those with oily hair who want a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo and conditioner that can balance scalp oil without stripping moisture. Also great for individuals with sensitive scalps or color-treated hair looking for a premium, restorative product.
