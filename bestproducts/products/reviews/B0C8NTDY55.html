With its sleek Dazzle Steel finish and innovative features, this refrigerator combines aesthetics and functionality seamlessly.

Key Features:

  • Inverter Technology: The Smart Inverter Compressor adjusts cooling according to the load, which not only saves energy but also enhances durability and reduces noise.
  • Capacity: With a capacity of 322 liters, it provides sufficient space for families, allowing for organized storage of food and beverages.
  • Frost-Free Operation: The frost-free design eliminates the need for manual defrosting, making maintenance effortless and keeping food fresher longer.

  • Express Freeze Function: This feature quickly freezes items, making it ideal for ice production and preserving fresh foods.
  • Smart Connectivity: With smart features, you can monitor and control the refrigerator settings through your smartphone, adding convenience to your kitchen routine.
  • Design: The Dazzle Steel finish not only enhances the refrigerator's aesthetic appeal but also resists fingerprints and is easy to clean.

Pros:
