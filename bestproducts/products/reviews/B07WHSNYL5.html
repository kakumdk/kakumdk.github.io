Pros:

Cons:

  • Plastic Build: Lacks the premium feel of metal or glass designs.
  • No Expandable Storage: Limited to 128GB with no SD card slot.
  • Average Low-Light Photography: Camera struggles slightly in extreme low light.
  • No IP Rating: Not certified for water or dust resistance.

  • No Stereo Speakers: Mono audio lacks depth.

  • No Wireless Charging: Only supports wired charging.
  • Battery Capacity: Could have been slightly larger for heavy users.

Verdict:

The iQOO Z9 Lite 5G is a well-rounded smartphone offering solid performance with the Dimensity 6300 5G processor, a 50MP Sony AI Camera, and 6GB RAM. The inclusion of a charger in the box is a welcome addition. While it may lack some premium features like wireless charging and water resistance, it provides excellent value for money, making it an ideal choice for users looking for a budget-friendly yet powerful 5G smartphone.

Best For: Budget-conscious buyers who need reliable performance, great camera quality, and 5G support.
