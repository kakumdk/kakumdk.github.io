Comprehensive Review:

Key Features:

  • 12GB LPDDR5 RAM & 256GB UFS 3.1 Storage: Fast performance and ample space.
  • 6.74-inch 1.5K 120Hz AMOLED Display: Crisp, smooth visuals with vibrant colors.
  • Snapdragon 7+ Gen 3 Processor: Flagship-level performance and efficiency.
  • 50MP Sony AI Camera System: With OIS and advanced photography features.
  • 16MP Front Camera: Clear and sharp selfies.
  • 5500mAh Battery with 80W SUPERVOOC Charging: Charges up in less than 30 minutes.
  • OxygenOS 14 (Android 14): Smooth, near-stock Android experience.
  • In-Display Fingerprint Sensor: Fast and secure unlocking.
  • 5G Dual SIM Support: Next-gen connectivity.
  • Stereo Speakers with Dolby Atmos: Immersive audio experience.
  • IP65 Water & Dust Resistance: Durable for daily use.

Pros:

  • Powerful Snapdragon 7+ Gen 3: Smooth performance, gaming-ready.
  • High-Quality 120Hz AMOLED Display: Great for media consumption.
  • Long Battery Life: Lasts over a day with moderate usage.
  • Super Fast Charging (80W): Zero to full in under 30 minutes.
  • Clean & Smooth OxygenOS 14: No bloatware, fast updates.
  • Great Stereo Speakers: Dolby Atmos enhances the experience.
  • Premium Design & Build Quality: Feels solid and premium in hand.
  • Reliable 50MP Camera: AI-enhanced photography delivers great shots.
  • 5G Future-Proofing: Strong network performance.
  • IP65 Rating: Better water resistance than previous Nord models.

Cons:

  • No Wireless Charging: Expected in this price range.
  • No Telephoto Lens: Zoom quality is limited to digital.
  • No Expandable Storage: 256GB is enough for most, but no microSD support.
  • OIS Only on Main Camera: Ultrawide and front cameras lack stabilization.
  • Pre-Installed OnePlus Apps: Minimal but still present.
  • Lacks a 3.5mm Headphone Jack: Need Type-C or wireless headphones.
  • No Charger in the Box (EU Models): Some regions get the charger, others don’t.

Verdict:

The OnePlus Nord 4 5G is a feature-packed upper-midrange phone that delivers flagship-like performance, a beautiful display, excellent battery life, and super-fast charging. With Snapdragon 7+ Gen 3, OxygenOS 14, and a 50MP Sony AI-enhanced camera, it’s one of the best midrange 5G phones in 2025. While it lacks wireless charging and a telephoto lens, it makes up for it with speed, smoothness, and a premium build.

Best For: Users looking for a powerful midrange phone with a smooth OxygenOS experience, excellent performance, fast charging, and a high-quality display.
