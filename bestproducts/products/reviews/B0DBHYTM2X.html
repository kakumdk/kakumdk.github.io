Comprehensive Review

Key Features:

  • Dual Drivers (11mm + 6mm): Dual driver setup for a balanced sound profile with deep bass, clear mids and crisp highs, enhancing the overall listening experience.
  • Dual DACs (Digital-to-Analog Converters): Enhances audio quality by providing better sound conversion, resulting in higher fidelity.
  • Dynaudio EQs: Sound profiles tuned by Dynaudio, with customizable EQ settings to adjust sound to your preference.
  • Adaptive Noise Cancellation (Up to 50dB): Blocks out external noise with up to 50dB of adaptive ANC, which adjusts according to your environment.
  • Battery Life: Up to 43 hours of total battery life with the charging case (9 hours from the buds alone with ANC off).
  • Fast Charging: 10 minutes of charging provides up to 10 hours of playback, making them convenient for quick top-ups.
  • Bluetooth 5.3: Offers stable connectivity, low latency and high-quality sound streaming.
  • IP55 Rating: Dust and water resistance, making them suitable for workouts and outdoor use.
  • Sleek Design: Comes in the premium Lunar Radiance color, offering a stylish and modern look.

Pros:

  • Excellent Sound Quality: The dual drivers and DACs provide rich, detailed sound with a wide frequency range, perfect for audiophiles and casual listeners alike.
  • Adaptive Noise Cancellation: The ANC feature is highly effective in most environments, making these buds ideal for commutes, work, or even workouts.
  • Long Battery Life: With up to 43 hours of total playback time and quick charging, these buds are great for long days or travel.
  • Dynaudio Tuning: Customizable sound profiles with Dynaudio EQs allow for personalized sound preferences, enhancing the listening experience for different music genres.
  • Fast and Stable Connectivity: Bluetooth 5.3 provides a strong connection and seamless pairing, especially with OnePlus devices.
  • Comfortable Fit: The ergonomic design ensures the buds stay in place even during physical activity, with a secure and comfortable fit.
  • Durable Design: With an IP55 rating, the buds can withstand sweat and light splashes, making them suitable for workouts and outdoor activities.

Cons:

  • Price: Positioned in the premium category, the price may be higher than some competitors, though the features justify the cost for many users.
  • ANC in Extreme Environments: While the ANC is strong, it might not completely eliminate very loud noises in environments like planes or construction sites.
  • Slightly Bulky: Some users found the buds to be a bit larger than expected, which could be uncomfortable for those with smaller ears.
  • Limited ANC Customization: Though adaptive, the noise cancellation settings don’t offer as much manual customization as some competitors in the premium market.

Verdict:

The OnePlus Buds Pro 3 are a premium pair of wireless earbuds designed for audiophiles and everyday users who want excellent sound quality, advanced features and long-lasting battery life. The dual drivers, dual DACs and Dynaudio-tuned EQs deliver a refined and customizable listening experience, while the adaptive noise cancellation keeps unwanted sounds at bay.

Though the buds are slightly bulkier than expected, their ergonomic fit, long battery life and fast charging make them ideal for both daily use and travel. The price point reflects the high-end features, but if you're looking for superior sound quality, ANC and overall performance, these earbuds are well worth the investment.

Ideal For: Audiophiles, frequent travelers and users looking for premium wireless earbuds with great sound quality and noise cancellation.
