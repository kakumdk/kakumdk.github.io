The FYA Handheld Vacuum Cleaner is a versatile 2-in-1 device that combines powerful suction capabilities with air-blowing functionality. Designed for portability and convenience, this mini vacuum is perfect for quick clean-ups at home or in your car.

Key Features:

  • Powerful Suction: Despite its compact size, the vacuum offers impressive suction power, making it effective for picking up dirt, debris and pet hair.
  • 2-in-1 Functionality: The vacuum can also function as an air blower, allowing for versatility in cleaning tasks, from vacuuming to blowing away leaves or dust.
  • Portable Design: Lightweight and cordless, it’s easy to handle and carry, making it suitable for various cleaning needs.

  • HEPA Filter: The inclusion of a HEPA filter ensures that allergens and fine dust are trapped, improving air quality during cleaning.

Pros:
