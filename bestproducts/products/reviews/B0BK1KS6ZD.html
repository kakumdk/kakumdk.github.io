Key Features:

  • 1.5 Ton Capacity: Ideal for medium-sized rooms, providing efficient cooling without overworking the unit.
  • 3 Star Energy Rating: Balances performance and energy efficiency, helping to keep electricity costs manageable.
  • Inverter Technology: Adjusts the cooling output based on room temperature, reducing power consumption and maintaining a consistent climate.
  • PM 2.5 Filter: Effectively filters out harmful particulate matter, improving indoor air quality.
  • Dew Clean Technology: Simplifies maintenance by facilitating easy cleaning of the indoor unit.
  • Coanda Airflow: Distributes air evenly across the room, preventing cold spots and ensuring a comfortable environment.
  • Triple Display: Shows temperature settings, mode and other functions for easy monitoring.

Pros:

  • Efficient Cooling: Users report quick and effective cooling, making it perfect for hot climates.
  • Improved Air Quality: The PM 2.5 filter is a significant advantage for those with allergies or respiratory issues.
