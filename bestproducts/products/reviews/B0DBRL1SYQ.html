Cons:

  • Limited Viewing Angles: Picture quality may degrade slightly when viewed from the sides.
  • Average App Responsiveness: Certain apps may take longer to load on Google TV.
  • No HDR10+ Support: Lacks HDR10+ compatibility, which some competing models offer.
  • Mediocre Remote Design: Could feel less premium compared to high-end remotes.
