The Classic Mosquito Net for Double Bed (King Size) is designed to provide a protective barrier against mosquitoes and other insects while ensuring a comfortable sleeping environment. Key Features:

  • King Size Dimensions: Specifically designed to fit double and king-size beds, offering ample coverage for a comfortable sleeping space.
  • Foldable Design: The net can be easily set up or taken down, making it convenient for use at home or while traveling.
  • Strong Polyester Material: Made from 30GSM polyester, this net is both lightweight and durable, ensuring long-lasting performance.
  • PVC Coated Corrosion Resistant Steel Wire: The frame is constructed with steel wire coated in PVC, providing strength and resistance to corrosion for added durability.
  • Effective Insect Protection: Fine netting effectively blocks mosquitoes and other pests, promoting a peaceful and undisturbed sleep.

Pros:
