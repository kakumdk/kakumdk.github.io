Comprehensive Review:

Key Features:

  • 750 Watts Powerful Motor: Equipped with a 750W motor for powerful grinding and blending, suitable for a wide range of tasks including grinding spices, making batter and blending.
  • 4 Jars Included: Comes with 3 stainless steel jars and 1 juicer jar for various purposes:
    • Small Jar for grinding spices.
    • Medium Jar for making chutneys or pastes.
    • Large Jar for blending and mixing larger quantities.
    • Juicer Jar with a strainer for extracting juices.
  • Stainless Steel Blades: Fitted with super-efficient stainless steel blades in each jar, ensuring quick and smooth grinding and mixing.
  • Multiple Speed Settings: Features 3-speed control with an in-built pulse function for more control over the grinding and blending process.
  • Sturdy Build: Designed with stainless steel jars and durable plastic body, ensuring long-lasting performance.
  • Overload Protection: Comes with a built-in overload protection feature that safeguards the motor from overheating during heavy use.
  • Compact and Space-Saving: Designed to fit well in modern kitchens without taking up too much counter space.
  • 2-Year Warranty: Includes a 2-year warranty on the product, giving peace of mind and assurance of quality.

Pros:

  • Powerful Motor: The 750W motor ensures that even tough ingredients like spices, nuts and grains are ground smoothly without much effort.
  • Versatile Use: The 4 different jars provide versatility in the kitchen, allowing users to blend, grind and juice with ease. Perfect for a variety of cooking tasks such as making chutneys, smoothies and grinding masalas.
  • Efficient Blades: The stainless steel blades in the jars ensure quick and efficient grinding, reducing preparation time in the kitchen.
  • Durable Jars: The stainless steel jars are sturdy, rust-resistant and easy to clean, ensuring long-lasting performance.
  • Compact Design: The mixer grinder is compact, making it suitable for kitchens with limited space while still offering powerful performance.
  • Juicer Functionality: The included juicer jar is a great addition for making fresh juice, making this a multifunctional appliance.
  • Affordable Price: Offers good value for money, especially considering the multiple jars and powerful motor, which makes it a great buy for households looking for an all-in-one solution.
  • Safety Features: The overload protection feature ensures that the motor remains safe during extended use, preventing damage from overheating.

Cons:

  • Noise Level: As with most powerful mixer grinders, this model can be quite noisy, especially when grinding harder ingredients or running on higher speeds.
  • Juicer Jar Lid: Some users report issues with the juicer jar lid, saying it doesn’t fit tightly and can lead to spillage if not handled carefully.
  • Plastic Build: While the jars are durable, some users feel the plastic components, such as the lid and body, could be sturdier.
  • Motor Heating: The motor may heat up slightly if used continuously for extended periods, so it’s best to give it a break between long sessions.
  • Cleaning the Juicer Jar: The juicer jar can be a bit tricky to clean, especially if you’re using fibrous fruits or vegetables.

Verdict:

The Prestige Iris Plus 750 W Mixer Grinder is a versatile and powerful kitchen appliance that offers excellent value for money. With its 750W motor and four jars, it’s suitable for a wide variety of tasks such as grinding, blending and juicing, making it a great all-in-one solution for home cooking needs. The stainless steel jars and efficient blades make grinding and blending quick and easy, while the juicer jar is an added bonus for those who enjoy making fresh juices at home.

However, the noise level and potential spillage from the juicer jar lid might be drawbacks for some users, especially those who prefer quieter operation or frequently use the juicer function. The plastic components also may not feel as premium as the rest of the machine. Overall, it’s a great option for small to medium households that need a reliable and efficient mixer grinder for everyday cooking.

Ideal For: Households looking for a multifunctional mixer grinder that can handle a variety of tasks, from grinding masalas and chutneys to blending juices and batters. Perfect for those who need a powerful yet compact kitchen appliance at an affordable price.
