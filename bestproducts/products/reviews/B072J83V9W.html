Key Features

2-Litre Capacity: The generous 2-liter capacity allows you to make large batches of juice, making it ideal for families or entertaining guests.

Powerful Motor: Equipped with a strong motor, this juicer quickly extracts juice from a variety of fruits and vegetables, ensuring you get the maximum yield with minimal effort.

Wide Feeding Tube: The large feeding tube can accommodate whole fruits, reducing the need for pre-cutting and saving valuable prep time.

Built-in Froth Separator: This feature helps separate froth from juice, allowing you to enjoy a smoother, more enjoyable drinking experience.
