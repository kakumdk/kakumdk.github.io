Key Features:

  • Snapdragon 6 Gen 1 Processor: Efficient and powerful performance with 560K+ AnTuTu score.
  • 6000mAh Battery: Long-lasting endurance for heavy users.
  • 44W FlashCharge: Quick charging for minimal downtime.
  • 6.72-inch FHD+ Display: 120Hz refresh rate for smooth visuals.

Pros:

  • Powerful Battery: 6000mAh ensures all-day usage.
  • Fast Charging: 44W FlashCharge is quick and efficient.
  • Smooth Performance: Snapdragon 6 Gen 1 handles multitasking well.
  • 120Hz Display: Smooth scrolling and fluid animations.
  • Lightweight Design: Only 7.99mm thick despite the large battery.
  • Good Stereo Speakers: Clear and loud audio.
  • 5G Support: Future-ready for high-speed connectivity.
  • Expandable Storage: Supports microSD card for extra space.
  • Stylish Design: Storm Grey looks premium.
  • Side Fingerprint Sensor: Quick and reliable unlocking.

Cons:

  • Only 4GB RAM: Might struggle with heavy multitasking.
  • Bloatware Present: Some pre-installed apps that need manual removal.
  • No AMOLED Display: Uses LCD instead of OLED for deeper blacks.
  • No Ultra-Wide Camera: Limited camera versatility.
  • Plastic Build: Lacks a premium feel.
  • Average Low-Light Photography: Struggles in dim lighting.
  • No Wireless Charging: Expected at a higher price range.
  • No HDR Support: Not ideal for HDR streaming.
  • No IP68 Water Resistance: Only basic splash protection.
  • No In-Display Fingerprint Sensor: Side-mounted instead.

Verdict:

The iQOO Z9x 5G is an excellent budget-friendly 5G smartphone with a massive battery, smooth 120Hz display, and fast Snapdragon 6 Gen 1 processor. While it lacks an AMOLED display and ultra-wide camera, it excels in battery life, charging speed, and overall performance.

Best For: Users looking for a long-lasting battery, fast performance, and a smooth display in a budget-friendly 5G smartphone.
