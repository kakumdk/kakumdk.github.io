The Fire-Boltt Ninja Call Pro Plus Smart Watch is designed for those seeking a blend of advanced features and style. With a 46.48mm (1.83 inch) display and packed with functionality, this smartwatch caters to fitness enthusiasts and tech-savvy users alike.

Key Features:

  • Large Display: The 1.83-inch high-resolution screen (240 x 280 pixels) offers vibrant visuals and easy navigation, enhancing the user experience.
  • Bluetooth Calling: Make and receive calls directly from your wrist, ensuring you stay connected hands-free during workouts or daily activities.
  • AI Voice Assistance: Control your watch and connected devices effortlessly with voice commands, making it convenient for multitasking.
  • Extensive Sports Modes: With 120+ sports modes available, this watch caters to a wide range of activities, allowing detailed tracking of your workouts.
  • IP67 Rating: This waterproof and dustproof rating makes it suitable for various environments, including workouts and outdoor activities.
