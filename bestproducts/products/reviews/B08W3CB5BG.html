The ElastPro Light Blue EVA Food Grade Washable Anti-Slip Mat/Sheet is designed to enhance organization and cleanliness in various areas of the home, particularly in kitchens and refrigerators. Made from food-grade EVA material, this mat provides a safe and functional solution for keeping items in place while preventing spills and messes.

Key Features:

  • Material: Constructed from high-quality EVA (Ethylene Vinyl Acetate), which is food-grade and safe for use in food storage areas.
  • Anti-Slip Design: The mat features a diamond texture that ensures items stay in place, minimizing slips and messes.
  • Washable: Easy to clean; simply wipe with a damp cloth or wash with soap and water to maintain hygiene.
  • Versatile Use: Suitable for use in fridges, shelves, kitchen drawers, or even on tables, making it a multifunctional addition to any home.
  • Customizable Size: Measures 45 cm x 1.5 m, but can be easily cut to fit specific spaces as needed.

Pros:

  • Durability: Users report that the mat is strong and holds up well over time, even with frequent cleaning.
  • Aesthetic Appeal: The light blue color adds a fresh and vibrant look to shelves and drawers.
  • Ease of Maintenance: Many customers appreciate how easy it is to clean, which enhances its practicality for everyday use.
  • Prevents Damage: Acts as a protective barrier against scratches and stains on shelves and surfaces.
