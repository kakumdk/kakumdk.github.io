  • 50MP + 8MP Ultrawide Camera: The dual-camera setup enables high-quality photography, with the 50MP main sensor delivering sharp images and the 8MP ultrawide camera offering versatile shooting options.
  • 16MP Front Camera: Ideal for selfies and video calls, the front camera captures clear and vibrant images.
