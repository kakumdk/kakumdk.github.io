  • MediaTek Dimensity 6080 Processor: This powerful processor ensures high performance and efficient power management, making it suitable for gaming and multitasking.
  • 120Hz FHD+ Display: The smooth 120Hz refresh rate offers an enhanced visual experience, allowing for fluid animations and better responsiveness during usage.
  • 50MP + 8MP Ultrawide Camera: The dual-camera setup enables high-quality photography, with the 50MP main sensor delivering sharp images and the 8MP ultrawide camera offering versatile shooting options.
  • 16MP Front Camera: Ideal for selfies and video calls, the front camera captures clear and vibrant images.
  • 33W Fast Charging: The fast charging feature ensures that users can quickly recharge their devices, minimizing downtime.
  • Expandable RAM: Starting with 8GB of RAM, users have the option to expand up to 16GB, significantly boosting performance for demanding applications.

Pros:

  • Impressive Display: The 120Hz refresh rate enhances the user experience, making everything from scrolling to gaming feel more fluid.

  • Strong Performance: The combination of a capable processor and expandable RAM ensures smooth operation across all tasks.
