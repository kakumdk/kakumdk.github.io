  • Alarm Function: An anti-theft alarm triggers if someone tries to tamper with or move the safe.

Pros:

  • Secure Motorized Locking: Provides enhanced security over traditional manual locks.
  • Easy Installation and Use: Straightforward setup with clear instructions; keypad is intuitive.
  • Compact Yet Spacious: The 15-litre capacity is ideal for personal valuables without taking up much space.

Cons:

  • No Manual Override Option: Lack of a manual backup key could be a drawback if batteries fail.
  • Sensitive Alarm: The alarm feature can sometimes be triggered easily, which may be inconvenient in quiet spaces.
  • Battery Dependency: Needs a regular battery replacement for consistent functionality.
  • Basic Interior: Lacks internal padding, which may be a downside for users storing delicate items.
