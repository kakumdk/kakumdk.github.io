  • Set of 3 Mirrors: Each mirror measures 9 x 9 inches, allowing for versatile arrangements and displays, whether hung individually or as a group.
  • Colorful Frames: The multicolor design adds a cheerful and lively touch to any room, making these mirrors stand out as decorative pieces.
  • Material: Crafted with durable materials, the mirrors are designed to be both functional and aesthetically pleasing, ensuring long-lasting use.
  • Easy Installation: Comes with mounting hardware, making it simple to hang and arrange the mirrors according to your preference.

Pros:

  • Vibrant Aesthetic: Users love the colorful frames, which help brighten up their spaces and add personality to the decor.
  • Versatile Use: Suitable for various rooms, including living rooms, bedrooms and entryways, offering flexibility in design.
