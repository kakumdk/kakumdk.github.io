  3. Powder-Coated Finish: The black powder-coated finish gives it a sleek and modern look while protecting it from rust or corrosion.
  4. Durable Material: Made from high-quality, durable metal, ensuring long-lasting use even in humid environments like bathrooms.
  5. Space-Saving Design: Compact yet spacious enough to store a variety of items, helping to declutter your space efficiently.
  6. Load Capacity: Can hold a considerable amount of weight, making it practical for storing multiple items without sagging or falling.

Pros:

  • No Drilling Required: Adhesive installation makes it easy to install without causing damage to walls.

  • Easy to Clean: The design and material make it easy to wipe clean, ensuring it stays neat and tidy.
