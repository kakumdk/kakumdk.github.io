Pros:

  • Digital Temperature Display: The digital display is a convenient feature, allowing users to see the exact temperature of the water at any given time.

  • Compact and Aesthetic Design: The sleek white design adds a touch of modernity to bathrooms without taking up much space.

  • No Timer: There’s no timer function to schedule heating cycles, which could be a downside for users who want to pre-set the heating times.
  • Requires Professional Installation: Installation may require professional help, especially in high-rise buildings and the cost may not always be included in the purchase.

Verdict:

The V-Guard Divino DG Geyser 15 Litre Water Heater is a fantastic option for families looking for an efficient, reliable and safe water heater, especially in areas with hard water or for those living in high-rise buildings. Its 5-star energy rating ensures cost savings on electricity, while the digital display adds a level of convenience for monitoring water temperature.

The advanced 4-layer safety system makes it a highly secure choice, offering protection from overheating and high water pressure. While the lack of smart features like remote control or timers might be a slight drawback, the fast heating capability and stylish design more than make up for it.
