Pros:

  • Energy Efficient: The 5-star rating ensures that the geyser consumes minimal electricity, making it cost-effective in the long run.
  • Digital Temperature Display: The digital display is a convenient feature, allowing users to see the exact temperature of the water at any given time.

Cons:

  • No Timer: There’s no timer function to schedule heating cycles, which could be a downside for users who want to pre-set the heating times.

Verdict:

The V-Guard Divino DG Geyser 15 Litre Water Heater is a fantastic option for families looking for an efficient, reliable and safe water heater, especially in areas with hard water or for those living in high-rise buildings.
