The Butterfly Cordial 3 Litres Pressure Cooker is designed to provide efficient and reliable cooking for modern households. With its durable construction and user-friendly features, this pressure cooker is perfect for preparing a variety of dishes quickly and safely.

Key Features:

  • 3 Litres Capacity: Ideal for small to medium-sized families, allowing for versatile cooking without taking up too much space.
  • Outer Lid Design: This design makes it easy to operate, providing convenience when adding or removing food.
  • Food Grade Virgin Aluminium: Constructed from high-quality aluminum, ensuring even heat distribution for faster cooking and better flavor retention.
  • Induction & Gas Stove Compatible: Versatility in cooking methods allows it to be used on both induction and gas stoves.
  • ISI Certified: Meets quality and safety standards, providing assurance for safe cooking.
  • 2 Years Manufacturer's Warranty: Offers peace of mind regarding durability and performance.

Pros:

  • Efficient Cooking: Users appreciate the quick cooking times, saving energy and reducing meal prep time.
  • Lightweight & Easy to Handle: The design is user-friendly, making it easy to maneuver and manage during cooking.
  • Versatile Use: Suitable for various cooking styles, from curries to rice and lentils.
  • Durable Construction: Built to last, providing reliable performance over time.

  • Limited Capacity for Larger Families: Some users might find the 3-litre size small for larger gatherings or meal prep.
