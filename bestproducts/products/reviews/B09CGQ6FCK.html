Pros:

  • Advanced Purification Technology: RO, UV, and UF provide thorough purification, effectively handling high TDS levels and removing impurities.
  • Low Maintenance: Four years of free service reduces ongoing costs, making it budget-friendly in the long run.
  • High Storage Capacity: The 8L tank ensures a steady supply, even during power outages, ideal for medium-sized households.
  • Customizable TDS Levels: TDS Control feature allows users to adjust the level of essential minerals in water, catering to personal taste preferences.
  • Durable Build and Design: Built with sturdy materials, it complements modern kitchens while being reliable.
  • ISI Certified: Provides additional assurance of water safety and quality compliance.

Cons:

  • Bulky Design: Larger size may not fit well in compact kitchens or small spaces.
  • High Initial Cost: Slightly expensive upfront, though compensated by the free service offer.
  • Needs Regular Power Supply: Requires electricity to operate, which may be inconvenient in areas with frequent power cuts.

  • Exchange Offer Limitations: Only available in select locations, limiting availability for some buyers.

Verdict:

The KENT Grand RO Water Purifier is a comprehensive solution for families seeking pure drinking water with minimal maintenance.
