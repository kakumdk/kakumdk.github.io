  • Affordable Pricing: Offers advanced features at a reasonable price point.

Cons:

  • No Incline Adjustment: Limited to flat running surface; lacks incline for more challenging workouts.
  • Massager Power: Could be more powerful for deeper muscle relaxation.
  • App Dependency: Some features require the Fit Show app, which might not appeal to all users.
  • Weight Limit: While adequate, users heavier than 110kg cannot use it safely.
  • Compact Tread Surface: May feel small for taller users or those with longer strides.
  • No Cooling Fan: Lacks built-in fans for user comfort during longer sessions.
  • Limited Warranty: Shorter motor or part warranties compared to premium models.
  • Speed Limit: Advanced runners might find the 14km/hr maximum speed limiting.
  • No Bluetooth Connectivity: Limits wireless integrations and external media usage.
  • Bulky When Unfolded: Takes up significant space during use.

Verdict:

The MAXPRO PTM405M treadmill is an excellent choice for home fitness enthusiasts seeking a multifunctional machine with features like a massager and app support. Its foldable design, sturdy build, and intuitive LED display make it user-friendly and practical for everyday use. However, advanced users may miss features like incline adjustment and higher speed settings.

Best For: Beginners and intermediate users looking for a reliable, feature-packed treadmill for regular workouts at home.
