Key Features

Helio G99 Processor: This efficient 6nm processor ensures smooth multitasking and gaming performance, making it suitable for demanding apps.

Massive 6000mAh Battery: With an extraordinary battery life, users can enjoy extended usage without frequent recharges, ideal for heavy users.

90Hz Refresh Rate: The high refresh rate provides a smooth scrolling experience, enhancing gameplay and general navigation.

50MP Rear Camera: Capture stunning images with the 50MP camera, which performs well in various lighting conditions.

Generous Storage: With 8GB RAM and 128GB storage, the POVA 4 provides ample space for apps, photos and multimedia.

Pros
