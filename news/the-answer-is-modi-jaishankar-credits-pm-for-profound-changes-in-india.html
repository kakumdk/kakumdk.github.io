'The Answer is Modi': Jaishankar on what has changed in India

External affairs minister S Jaishankar on Monday reflected on the prof

External affairs minister S Jaishankar on Monday reflected on the profound changes in India over the past decade, attributing them to the leadership of Prime Minister Narendra Modi. Addressing a Diwali reception in London, the minister emphasized the pivotal role played by PM Modi in shaping the nation's trajectory. The minister elaborated on the multitude of initiatives that have shaped India's progress over the past decade. He highlighted key campaigns such as Beti Padhao, Beti Bachao (Educate the Daughter, Save the Daughter), the construction of toilets for girls, the Jandhan Yojana for financial inclusion, the Awas Yojana for housing and flagship projects like Digital India, Startup India and Skill India.
