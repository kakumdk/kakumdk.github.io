ISRO EOS 08 launch highlights: The Indian Space Research Organisation

ISRO EOS 08 launch highlights: The Indian Space Research Organisation launches third developmental flight of Small Satellite Launch Vehicle (SSLV) from the Satish Dhawan Space Centre in Sriharikota, Andhra Pradesh at 9:17am on Friday. What are mission objectives of ISRO’s SSLV-D3/EOS-08 mission? To demonstrate reliable and consistent performance of the SSLV vehicle systems.

To successfully place the EOS-08 satellite and SR-0 DEMOSAT into a 475 km circular orbit.

Where to watch ISRO EOS-08 mission live?

The launch of the third developmental flight of SSLV can be watched live on Friday from 08:50am on ISRO's social media handles. You can access the platforms via the provided links or join through the window below.
