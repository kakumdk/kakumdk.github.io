Fighting in a Couple's Relationship is Normal

04 December, 2020 by Karthik
Read Article Pause Resume Stop

Every couple will fight and it's completely normal, unless its scares both of them. No two peoples are exact same way, each of them are unique. Couple's can occasionally or often might have conflicts or disagreements which might lead to argument of fight. If that conflict of argument is taken as healthy discussion then you gona know more about your partner and you both will surely work as a team.

As I see, their are few advantages of having such fights. like..

You care a lot: You are making a heathy argument, means you are more committed and you will care a lot.
You communicate easily: It's kind of Trust in your relationship, you will not be quite and you will communicate it, like whatever it is.
You will have a strong bond: In the arguments, you get a chance to learn more about each other, that will strengthen your bond with each other.
You have a mature relationship: Means, If you are tell your thoughts clearly while arguing, their will be a chance for compromise and have improvements in the relationship.
Your relationship will be healthy: If you are never arguing means, their is something wrong. Arguments will help in reconsidering the feelings/expectations on each other.
You will stay together for long: A good reason for not living together is not having a reasonable communication, So if you are have a healthy argument means you are staying together for long time.

As I see, fighting in a relationship is normal and even good too.. Below are few tips to keep the argument healthy..

  • Respect each other, Stick to the point.
  • Express your curiosity, Discuss the emotional cause of the issue
  • Apologize, Begin carefully.
  • Listen, You should be a good listener, listen first & then act.
  • Very important, Avoid making demands or engaging in disrespect or lashing out in anger.
  • Finally Discuss the past feelings of affection & love.

Having fight in a couples relationship is quite normal, if a couple told me they never fight, then i would think their is some issue.

Let's say, these fights between couple are going indefinitely, i mean like starting fights, involving your children or making threats, or constantly bringing up other peoples comparison into your relationship or physical abuse, then really some others help is need to sort it out and get your relationship on to track.
