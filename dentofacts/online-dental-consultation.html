Why Choose Online Consultation?

  • Convenient: Avoid long clinic waits and consult at your preferred time.
  • Expert Advice: Direct consultation with Dr. Jyothi Karthik D, a trusted and experienced dentist.
  • Confidential: Your details and discussions are handled with utmost privacy.
  • Affordable: Save time and money with a hassle-free virtual visit.

How It Works

  1. Book an Appointment: Select an available slot that works best for you.
  2. Provide Details: Share your name, contact details, and a brief description of your concern.
  3. Join the Consultation: Connect via video or audio at the scheduled time.
  4. Receive Expert Guidance: Get a professional assessment and a personalized treatment plan.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What can I expect during the consultation?
A: Dr. Jyothi will discuss your symptoms, review any photos/X-rays, and provide advice or a treatment plan.

Q: Is this consultation suitable for emergencies?
A: For severe emergencies, it’s recommended to visit a clinic or hospital immediately. However, Dr. Jyothi can guide you on the next steps.

Q: How much does the consultation cost?
A: Pricing information is available during the appointment booking process.
