Sanjeev Sharma is a former Indian cricketer known for his contributions to the game during the 1970s and 1980s. As a prominent fast bowler, Sharma's career in domestic and international cricket is remembered for his skillful performances and dedication to the sport.

Early Life and Background

  • Birth and Family:
    • Sanjeev Sharma was born on September 17, 1948, in Delhi, India.
    • Growing up in a cricket-loving environment, he developed an early interest in the sport, which would shape his future career.
  • Education:

    • His involvement in school and local cricket teams provided him with valuable experience and exposure to the game.

Domestic Cricket Career

  • Domestic Debut:
