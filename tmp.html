Prerequisite

  • Prep time: 30 minutes
  • Cook time: 15 minutes
  • Number of servings: 4 persons

Ingredients

1. For the dough

  • Wheat flour 1 cup
  • Water
  • Oil
  • Salt to taste

2. For the egg stuffing

  • 2 Eggs
  • Onions 1
  • Green chili 2
  • Coriander leaves
  • Chili powder
  • Cumin powder
  • Garam masala
  • Chaat masala
  • Salt to taste

Method

  • In a bowl, add wheat flour, salt, oil and mix
  • Gradually add water and knead the dough
  • Divide the dough into equal portions
  • Place one of the portions onto a rolling surface and flatten it
  • Roll out the dough flat
  • Meanwhile, crack open the eggs into a glass/bowl, add all the ingredients for the stuffing
  • Mix everything well
  • Heat a tawa, place the rolled out chapati onto the tawa
  • Flip the chapati and cook on both sides for a couple of minutes
  • Make a slit on the chapati, create a pocket
  • Pour the egg mixture into the slit and seal it
  • Let the chapati cook and egg set inside
  • Apply ghee and cook on both sides till it is completely cooked
  • Cut the parathas and serve hot
