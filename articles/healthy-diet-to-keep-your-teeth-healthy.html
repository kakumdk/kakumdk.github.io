Healthy diet to keep your teeth healthy

By Jyothi Karthik D |
on 30th Oct 2023

For a healthy tooth a person will need vitamins, calcium, fluoride & phosphorus to be taken regularly in their diet.

Here are a few healthy foods & their contents that keep your teeth stronger. 

  • Vitamin A
    • Kale, Papaya, Carrot, Pumpkin.
  • Vitamin C
    • Oranges, Lemon, Cabbage, Cauliflower.
  • Vitamin D
    • Egg & Salmon Fish.
  • Calcium
    • Milk, Cheese, Yogurt.
  • Fluoride
    • Squid & Marina Fish.
  • Phosphorus
    • Liver & Mussel.

By having these vitamins, calcium, fluoride & phosphorus in your regular diet, you can make sure your oral health is at its best & healthy.
