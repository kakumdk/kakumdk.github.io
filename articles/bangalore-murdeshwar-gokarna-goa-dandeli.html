Bangalore to Murdeshwar, Gokarna, Goa and Dandeli Solo Trip Plan

On23rd Jun 2020, 2024-11-28T08:27:04+05:30 ByKarthik Kumar D K | read
Listen Pause Resume Stop

Ten days Ride from Bangalore Murdeshwar Gokarna Goa Dandeli Bangalore, If you are a Couple riding on Bike or You are riding with a Friend in two different Bikes, then this plan for ten days works very well. Below is the route mentioned where you can plan accordingly, if at all you need to spend some more time in some place in the list, you can skip the next place in the planned route.

Route

Bangalore Murdeshwar Mirjan Fort Sri Mahabaleshwara Swamy Temple Om Beach Half Moon Beach Paradise Beach Palolem Beach Butterfly Beach Patnem Beach Cola Beach Colva Beach Aguada Fort Panjim Calangute Anjuna Beach Vagator Beach Arambol Beach Dandeli Bangalore

1. Bangalore Murdeshwar:
  • Distance: 9h - 10h (500 km)
  • Leave Bangalore in the morning, 5:00 / 5:30 so that you reach somewhere between 3:30 - 4:00.
  • Take rest, have dinner with some Sea food.
  • Leave to Murdeshwar Temple in the early morning, you can spend time for 3h - 4h.
  • Have Breakfast around 10:00 - 10:30.
  • Leave to the next destination.
2. Murdeshwar Mirjan Fort, Gokarna:
  • Distance: 1h - 1h 20min (60 km)
  • Time you can Spend: 2 - 2.5 hours
  • This beautiful piece of architecture comes alive in all its greenery.
  • Have Lunch around 2:00 - 2:30.
3. Mirjan Fort, Gokarna Sri Mahabaleshwara Swamy Temple, Gokarna:
  • Distance: 30min - 40min (20 km)
  • Time you can Spend: 1.5 - 2 hours
  • Shiva linga at Mahabaleshwar Temple is one of the most vital.
4. Sri Mahabaleshwara Swamy Temple, Gokarna Om Beach & Half Moon Beach & Paradise Beach, Gokarna:
  • Distance: 20 min (10 km)
  • Take rest, stay nearby hotel of Om Beach.
  • Spend an evening walk at the Om Beach
  • Next day morning post breakfast, get on to a Trail towards Half Moon beach
  • Its 1 kilometer, you will reach by 10:00 -10:30, Spend 2 - 3 hours in the beach.
  • Next get on to a trail towards Paradise Beach, its 750 meters, you will reach by 2:00 - 2:30, Spend time for 2 - 3 hours.
  • Walk back to the hotel, via Half moon beach & Om beach, make sure you reach by sunset.
  • Have a sweet dinner on the beach side. and take rest to night.
5. Om Beach & Half Moon Beach & Paradise Beach, Gokarna Palolem, Goa:
  • Distance: 2:30 - 3:00 min (100 km)
  • Ride will be along the sea line, you will come across many beaches, Have breakfast on the ride.
  • Time you can Spend at Palolem Beach for 2-3 hours.
  • Their is one small beach Butterfly Beach nearby Palolem Beach 7.5 km
  • Visit to Butterfly Beach around 3:00, Spend time till sunset, before sunset come back towards Palolem Beach and go to Patnem Beach.
  • Stay near by Patnem Beach for this night and have a walk around 8:00 - 9:00 near beach.
6. Palolem, Goa Cola Beach Colva:
  • Next day morning ride towards, Cola Beach
  • Distance: 30 - 40 min (18 km)
  • Time you can Spend: 4 - 5 hours
  • You can swim here, you have a small fresh water lake kind of envirnoment. Really a nice place to spend some time more.
  • Leave towards Colva, Distance: 1h - 1h 15min (35 km)
  • Spend for some time in Colva Beach and Stay near by Colva for that night.
7. Colva Panjim:
  • Distance: 1h - 1h 15min (35 km)
  • Look around Aguada fort in the morning, Time you can Spend: 1 - 2 hours
  • Get the tickets for Casino for the evening and take rest till evening.
  • Get into the Casino in the evening around 7:00 or 8:00, Spend whole night in the Casino and leave Casino in the morning around 4:30 -5:30.
8. Panjim Calangute:
  • Next Day morning, move towards Calangute Beach and take rest till evening
  • Evening Candolim and Calungute Beach are bit good. Time you can Spend: 1 - 1.5 hours
  • Checkout the best deal for the Pub and get into pub by 6:00, spend till 11:00 their. and Take rest for that night.
9. Calangute Arambol:
  • Next Day morning, move towards Anjuna Beach, spend some time 1 - 2 hours.
  • Have sea food, Next move towards Vagator Beach, spend some couple of hours.
  • Reach Arambol by 4:00 or 5:00, Have a good evening in the Arambol Beach.
  • Spend that night in Arambol and Crowd is not disturbing. You can have a great Beach Party.
10. Arambol Dandeli:
  • Next Day morning, move towards Dandeli. Morning ride will be good.
  • Distance: 4h - 4h 30 min (160 km)
  • You can do river rafting and other water activities.
  • Spend that night in Dandeli, Take a complete rest.
11. Dandeli Bangalore:
  • Next Day morning, move towards Bangalore.
  • Distance: 9h - 10h (460 km)

Hope you like, Ten days Ride from Bangalore Murdeshwar Gokarna Goa Dandeli Bangalore. Its bit tight schedule, if you feel you need to spend some time in between you can skip the coming one and plan your trip accordingly.
