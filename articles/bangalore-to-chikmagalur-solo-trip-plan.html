Bangalore to Chikmagalur Solo Trip Plan

On27th Apr 2024, 2025-10-17T17:19:17+05:30 ByKarthik Kumar D K | read
Listen Pause Resume Stop

Solo trip from Bangalore to Chikmagalur promises a perfect blend of natural beauty, serene landscapes and rejuvenating experiences.

Here's a suggested itinerary for your solo trip:

Bangalore to Chikmagalur

  • Start your journey early in the morning to avoid traffic and reach Chikmagalur with ample time.
  • The distance between Bangalore and Chikmagalur is approximately 240 kilometers and the drive takes around 4-5 hours.
  • Check into your accommodation in Chikmagalur & Freshen up and take some rest after the journey.

Google Ad 1

  • Head to Mullayanagiri, the highest peak in Karnataka, for a trekking adventure.
  • Enjoy the scenic beauty and panoramic views from the summit.
  • Explore the lush coffee plantations for which Chikmagalur is famous.
  • Learn about the coffee-making process and enjoy a cup of freshly brewed coffee.

Explore Chikmagalur

  • Start your day with a visit to Kemmangundi, a hill station known for its beautiful gardens, waterfalls and scenic views.
  • Explore the landscaped gardens and take a nature walk.

Google Ad 2

  • Visit Hebbe Falls, surrounded by dense forests and coffee plantations.
  • Enjoy the mesmerizing beauty of the waterfall and take a refreshing dip in the natural pool.
  • Drive to Baba Budangiri, known for its picturesque landscapes and sunset views.
  • Witness the breathtaking sunset and soak in the peaceful ambiance.

Departure

  • Check out from your accommodation, En route to Bangalore, visit the historic temples of Belur and Halebidu, known for their exquisite architecture and intricate sculptures.
  • Start your journey back to Bangalore in the afternoon & Reach Bangalore by evening, concluding your solo trip to Chikmagalur.

Google Ad 3

Please note:

  • Check the weather conditions before your trip and plan outdoor activities accordingly.
  • Respect the local customs and traditions during your stay in Chikmagalur.
  • Ensure you have necessary permits and follow safety guidelines for trekking and waterfall visits.

This itinerary provides a general outline and you can adjust it based on your preferences and the time available. Always check for the latest travel advisories and conditions before embarking on your journey. Enjoy your solo trip to Chikmagalur.
