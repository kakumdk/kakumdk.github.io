Bangalore to Kudremukh Solo Trip Plan

On20th Nov 2023, 2024-12-03T18:33:12+05:30 ByKarthik Kumar D K | read
Listen Pause Resume Stop

Solo trip from Bangalore to Kudremukh promises an adventurous and nature-filled experience. Kudremukh is known for its scenic beauty, trekking trails and lush green landscapes.

The name Kuduremukha literally means "horse-faced" in Kannada and refers to a particular picturesque view of a side of the mountain that resembles a horse's face. It was also referred to as 'Samseparvata', historically since it was approached from Samse village. Kuduremukha is Karnataka's 2nd highest peak after Mullayanagiri and 26th highest peak in western ghats

Here's a suggested itinerary for your solo trip:

Route

Bangalore -> Hassan -> Belur -> Kalasa -> Kudremukh -> Hassan -> Bangalore

Bangalore to Kudremukh

  • Start your journey early in the morning to make the most of your day.
  • The distance between Bangalore and Kudremukh is approximately 350 kilometers and the drive takes around 7-8 hours.
  • Reach Kudremukh by late morning or early afternoon.
  • Check into your accommodation and freshen up.
  • Begin your adventure with a trek to the Kudremukh Peak, the third-highest peak in Karnataka.
  • Enjoy the through lush green landscapes and be rewarded with panoramic views from the top.
  • Spend the evening relaxing at your accommodation or exploring the nearby areas.
  • Enjoy the serene ambiance of Kudremukh.

Nature Exploration and Waterfalls

  • Take a nature walk in the beautiful surroundings of Kudremukh.
  • Explore the flora and fauna of the region.
  • Visit the nearby waterfalls like Hanuman Gundi Falls or Lakya Dam.
  • Enjoy the scenic beauty and take a refreshing dip if the waterfall allows.
  • Head to the Kudremukh Sunset Point for breathtaking views of the sunset.
  • Capture the beauty of the landscape during the golden hour.

Departure to Bangalore

  • Explore the Kudremukh National Park, known for its biodiversity.
  • Engage in birdwatching and enjoy the natural beauty.
  • Spend some leisure time, perhaps revisiting a favorite spot or exploring a local attraction.
  • Start your journey back to Bangalore in the afternoon.
  • Reach Bangalore by the evening, concluding your solo trip.

Please note:

  • Check the weather conditions before your trip, especially if you plan to trek.
  • Ensure you have the necessary permits if required for trekking in specific areas.
  • Respect the local environment and follow responsible tourism practices.

This itinerary provides a general outline and you can adjust it based on your preferences and the time available. Always check for the latest travel advisories and conditions before embarking on your journey. Enjoy your solo adventure in Kudremukh.
