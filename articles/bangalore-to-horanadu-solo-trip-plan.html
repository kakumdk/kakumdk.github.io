Bangalore to Horanadu Solo Trip Plan

On19th Oct 2023, 2025-03-24T07:53:23+05:30 ByKarthik Kumar D K | read
Listen Pause Resume Stop

Solo trip from Bangalore to Horanadu can be a serene and spiritual experience, as you'll get to visit the famous Horanadu Annapoorneshwari Temple and explore the natural beauty of the Western Ghats in Karnataka.

Here's a suggested itinerary for your trip:

Route

Bangalore -> Hassan -> Belur -> Mudigere -> Kalasa -> Horanadu -> Hassan -> Bangalore

Day 1: Bangalore to Horanadu

  • Start your trip early in the morning from Bangalore.
  • The distance between Bangalore and Horanadu is approximately 330 kilometers and it takes around 7-8 hours by road.
  • Take the route via Hassan and Mudigere to reach Horanadu.
  • Enjoy the scenic drive through the Western Ghats and the beautiful landscapes of Karnataka.
  • Reach Horanadu by late afternoon or early evening.
  • Check into your accommodation and take some time to relax and freshen up.
  • In the evening, you can visit the famous Horanadu Annapoorneshwari Temple and seek blessings.
  • Spend the night in Horanadu.

Day 2: Temple Visit and Nature Exploration

  • Start your day by visiting the Horanadu Annapoorneshwari Temple for the morning rituals and darshan.
  • Explore the temple premises and experience the spiritual ambiance.
  • In the afternoon, you can explore the nearby attractions like the Kudremukh National Park.
  • Enjoy the scenic beauty of the Western Ghats and the lush green forests.
  • If time permits, you can also visit the Kalasa town, located nearby and explore the Kalaseshwara Temple.
  • In the evening, you can relax amidst the serene beauty of Horanadu and enjoy the peaceful ambiance.
  • Spend another night in Horanadu.

Day 3: Departure

  • Start your day by visiting the nearby attractions in Horanadu.
  • You can visit the scenic Hanumanagundi Falls, located near Horanadu and enjoy the natural beauty.
  • Explore the beautiful landscapes and have a peaceful time in the lap of nature.
  • If time permits, you can also visit the Sringeri Sharada Peetham, one of the renowned Advaita Vedanta mathas in India, located near Horanadu.
  • In the afternoon, start your journey back to Bangalore.
  • Reach Bangalore by late evening and conclude your solo trip to Horanadu with beautiful memories.

Please note that this itinerary provides a general outline for your trip and you can modify it according to your preferences and the time you have available. Make sure to check the current travel guidelines, road conditions and weather forecasts before embarking on your journey.
