Bangalore to Alleppey Solo Trip Plan

On10th Aug 2023, 2025-03-20T14:06:34+05:30 ByKarthik Kumar D K | read
Listen Pause Resume Stop

Solo trip from Bangalore to Alleppey (Alappuzha) can be a memorable experience, as you'll get to explore the backwaters, houseboat cruises and serene beauty of the famous backwater destination in Kerala. Here's a suggested itinerary for your trip:

Route

Bangalore -> Salem -> Coimbatore -> Kochi -> Alleppey

Bangalore -> Maddur -> Chamarajanagar -> Coimbatore -> Kochi -> Alleppey

Day 1: Bangalore to Alleppey

  • Start your trip early in the morning from Bangalore.
  • The distance between Bangalore and Alleppey is approximately 590 kilometers and it takes around 10-11 hours by road.
  • Take the route via Salem, Coimbatore and Palakkad to reach Alleppey.
  • Enjoy the scenic drive through the Western Ghats and the beautiful countryside of Kerala.
  • Reach Alleppey by late afternoon or early evening.
  • Check into your accommodation and take some time to relax and freshen up.
  • In the evening, you can take a leisurely stroll along the Alleppey Beach and witness a beautiful sunset.
  • Spend the night in Alleppey.

Day 2: Backwater Experience

  • Start your day by experiencing the famous backwaters of Alleppey.
  • You can choose to go on a houseboat cruise or opt for a smaller, more traditional boat called a "shikara" ride.
  • Enjoy the scenic beauty of the backwaters, surrounded by coconut trees and paddy fields.
  • Observe the local life along the banks, including fishermen, villagers and various species of birds.
  • Savor a delicious traditional Kerala meal served on the houseboat.
  • If time permits, you can also visit attractions like the Krishnapuram Palace or the Ambalapuzha Sri Krishna Temple.
  • In the evening, you can take a walk around the Alleppey town and explore the local markets.
  • Spend another night in Alleppey.

Day 3: Departure

  • Start your day with a visit to the famous Alappuzha Lighthouse, which offers panoramic views of the surrounding area.
  • Explore the Alleppey Backwater Museum, where you can learn about the history and culture of the region.
  • If you're interested, you can participate in activities like canoeing or kayaking in the backwaters.
  • In the afternoon, start your journey back to Bangalore.
  • Reach Bangalore by late evening and conclude your solo trip to Alleppey with beautiful memories.

Please note that this itinerary provides a general outline for your trip and you can modify it according to your preferences and the time you have available. Make sure to check the current travel guidelines, road conditions and weather forecasts before embarking on your journey. Enjoy your solo adventure in Alleppey.
