Bangalore to Varkala Solo Trip Plan

On29th Jul 2023, 2025-01-14T08:41:06+05:30 ByKarthik Kumar D K | read
Listen Pause Resume Stop

A solo trip from Bangalore to Varkala can be a rejuvenating experience, as you'll get to explore the stunning beaches, cliffs and spiritual charm of this coastal town in Kerala.

Here's a suggested itinerary for your journey:

Route

Bangalore -> Salem -> Coimbatore -> Trivandrum -> Varkala -> Madurai -> Salem -> Bangalore

Bangalore -> Mysore -> Ooty -> Coimbatore -> Trivandrum -> Varkala -> Madurai -> Salem -> Bangalore

Day 1: Bangalore to Varkala

  • Start your trip early in the morning from Bangalore.
  • The distance between Bangalore and Varkala is approximately 680 kilometers and it takes around 12-13 hours by road.
  • Take the route via Salem, Coimbatore and Trivandrum to reach Varkala.
  • Enjoy the scenic drive through the Western Ghats and the coastal regions of Kerala.
  • Reach Varkala by late evening.
  • Check into your accommodation and take some time to relax and freshen up.
  • In the evening, you can visit the famous Varkala Cliff, where you'll find a range of cafes, shops and stunning views of the Arabian Sea.
  • Spend the night in Varkala.

Day 2: Exploring Varkala

  • Start your day by visiting the popular attractions in Varkala.
  • Begin with a visit to the stunning Varkala Beach, known for its unique cliffs and golden sands. You can relax on the beach, swim in the sea, or even try some water sports like surfing or parasailing.
  • Explore the Janardanaswamy Temple, a significant Hindu pilgrimage site dedicated to Lord Vishnu.
  • Visit the Sivagiri Mutt, the final resting place of the social reformer Sree Narayana Guru, which holds spiritual significance for the locals.
  • In the afternoon, you can take a boat ride in the backwaters of Varkala, surrounded by lush greenery and tranquility.
  • If time permits, you can also visit attractions like the Kappil Lake or the Anjengo Fort.
  • In the evening, enjoy a beautiful sunset view from the cliffs or indulge in some Ayurvedic treatments and yoga sessions available in Varkala.
  • Spend another night in Varkala.

Day 3: Beach Time and Departure

  • Start your day by spending some more time at the Varkala Beach.
  • Relax on the sands, take a dip in the sea and enjoy the peaceful ambiance.
  • Explore the different sections of the beach, such as the Papanasam Beach or the Black Sand Beach, each offering its own unique charm.
  • You can also visit the natural spring known as Varkala Tunnel, believed to have healing properties.
  • In the afternoon, start your journey back to Bangalore.
  • Reach Bangalore by late evening and conclude your solo trip to Varkala with beautiful memories.

Please note that this itinerary provides a general outline for your trip and you can modify it according to your preferences and the time you have available. Make sure to check the current travel guidelines, road conditions and weather forecasts before embarking on your journey. Enjoy your solo adventure in Varkala.
