Questions to ask Dentist before Dental Implant

By Jyothi Karthik D |
on 22nd Nov 2023

Here are a few important questions you need to remember and ask your dentist before you proceed with your dental implant.

  • What are the Dental Implant Steps?
  • Will there be any Discomfort?
  • How long Dental implants last?
  • Can my body reject Dental Implant?
  • Are there any chances of infection after Dental Implant?
  • Is there any need for Implant Rehabilitation?
