Brief Summary:

The Samsung Crystal 4K Vivid Pro and Vision Pro are part of Samsung's 2024 lineup of Crystal 4K TVs launched in India. These models offer various features aimed at enhancing the viewing experience.

Both models are designed to offer high-quality visuals and immersive sound, making them suitable for a modern smart home setup.
