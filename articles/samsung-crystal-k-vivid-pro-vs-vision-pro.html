Brief Summary:

The Samsung Crystal 4K Vivid Pro and Vision Pro are part of Samsung's 2024 lineup of Crystal 4K TVs launched in India. These models offer various features aimed at enhancing the viewing experience.

Both models are designed to offer high-quality visuals and immersive sound, making them suitable for a modern smart home setup.

In-Depth Review:

#1

Samsung 163 cm (65 inches) D Series Crystal 4K Vivid Pro Ultra HD Smart LED TV UA65DUE77AKLXL (Black)

Samsung Crystal 4K Vivid Pro:

  • Display: Features a 4K VA panel with a 60Hz refresh rate and HDR10+ support.
  • Processor: Equipped with the Crystal Processor 4K for better upscaling and performance.
  • Smart Features: Runs on Tizen OS, includes Samsung Daily+, and supports Alexa, Bixby, and Google Assistant.
  • Audio: Comes with 20W stereo speakers, OTS Lite for 3D sound effects, and Q-Symphony for enhanced audio when paired with compatible soundbars.
  • Connectivity: Offers three HDMI ports, one USB port, Wi-Fi, AirPlay, Smart View, and Bluetooth 5.3.
