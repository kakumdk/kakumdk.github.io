Best places in South India to travel in Hot Summer?

On19th Mar 2024, 2025-09-30T09:22:09+05:30 ByKarthik Kumar D K | read
Listen Pause Resume Stop

South India offers several destinations that are perfect for traveling during hot summers due to their cooler climates, hill stations and coastal areas.

Here are some of the best places to visit in South India during the hot summer months, along with reasons, ways to travel and the best time to visit:

  • Ooty, Tamil Nadu:

    • Why: Ooty, also known as the "Queen of Hill Stations," offers cool temperatures, lush greenery and scenic landscapes, making it an ideal escape from the summer heat.
    • How: Ooty is well-connected by road from major cities like Bangalore, Coimbatore and Mysore. You can also take a scenic train ride on the Nilgiri Mountain Railway.
    • When: The best time to visit Ooty is from March to June when the weather is pleasant and the flora is in full bloom.

  • Coorg, Karnataka:

    • Why: Coorg, known as the "Scotland of India," is blessed with misty hills, coffee plantations and cascading waterfalls, providing a refreshing retreat from the summer heat.
    • How: Coorg is accessible by road from Bangalore, Mysore and Mangalore. You can also take a scenic drive through the Western Ghats.
    • When: The best time to visit Coorg is from October to April when the weather is cool and pleasant.

  • Munnar, Kerala:

    • Why: Munnar is famous for its tea plantations, rolling hills and cool climate, offering respite from the summer heat and breathtaking views of the Western Ghats.
    • How: Munnar is well-connected by road from cities like Kochi, Coimbatore and Madurai. You can also hire a cab or take a bus from nearby towns.
    • When: The best time to visit Munnar is from September to May when the weather is cool and the tea gardens are at their lush green best.

  • Kodaikanal, Tamil Nadu:

    • Why: Kodaikanal, nestled in the Palani Hills, is known for its picturesque landscapes, serene lakes and pleasant climate, making it an ideal destination for summer vacations.
    • How: Kodaikanal is accessible by road from major cities like Bangalore, Chennai and Coimbatore. You can also take a scenic drive through the Western Ghats.
    • When: The best time to visit Kodaikanal is from April to June when the weather is cool and conducive for outdoor activities.

  • Wayanad, Kerala:

    • Why: Wayanad is known for its pristine forests, spice plantations and mist-clad mountains, providing a cool and tranquil environment amidst nature.
    • How: Wayanad is well-connected by road from cities like Bangalore, Kochi and Kozhikode. You can also take a scenic drive through the Western Ghats.
    • When: The best time to visit Wayanad is from October to May when the weather is pleasant and the natural beauty is at its peak.

These destinations in South India offer a perfect combination of cool weather, natural beauty and outdoor activities, making them ideal for a refreshing summer getaway.
