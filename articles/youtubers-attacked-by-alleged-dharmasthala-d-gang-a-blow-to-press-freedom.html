YouTubers Attacked by Alleged Dharmasthala D-Gang: A Blow to Press Freedom

On7th Aug 2025, 2025-09-21T10:17:01+05:30 ByKarthik Kumar D K | read
Listen Pause Resume Stop

Dharmasthala, Karnataka – 6th August 2025

In a shocking incident that has sparked widespread outrage, two independent YouTubers were reportedly attacked while covering ongoing developments around the mysterious deaths and criminal allegations in and around the Dharmasthala region. The attack, allegedly carried out by what locals and media sources are calling the "Dharmasthala D-Gang", raises serious questions about freedom of the press, safety of independent media voices, and the transparency of investigations linked to powerful figures.

The Incident

On 6th August 2025, members of YouTube channels Kudla Rampage and United Media were assaulted while filming near Dharmasthala. Eyewitnesses claim that a group of men surrounded the YouTubers, forcefully took away their equipment, and physically assaulted them. Videos and images from the hospital where they were admitted quickly circulated online, drawing attention from activists, media organizations, and the general public.

Who Is the 'D-Gang'?

The term "D-Gang" has increasingly become associated with a powerful group allegedly operating under the influence and protection of certain individuals linked to the Dharmasthala temple administration. While the name itself is unofficial, it is used by locals, independent media, and social media activists to describe the network allegedly involved in suppressing dissent, intimidating witnesses, and silencing victims—especially women.

This group is not a formally recognized entity, but its presence is deeply felt across the region. Over the years, numerous unnatural deaths, disappearances, and incidents of violence have been reported, many of which have either gone uninvestigated or ended without resolution.

Fear Among Locals

A climate of fear and silence dominates the Dharmasthala region. Locals, while deeply devoted to the temple, rarely speak out against any injustice they might witness or experience. Many believe that questioning the authority of the temple or those associated with it could lead to social ostracism, economic retaliation, or even physical harm.

  • Residents claim that informal surveillance and pressure tactics are used to discourage people from filing complaints or helping investigators.
  • Some who have tried to speak out about criminal activities have reportedly faced threats, job losses, and even mysterious disappearances.
  • Locals in the vicinity avoid talking to media personnel or strangers, often stating: “We don’t want trouble. It’s better to keep quiet.”

This culture of intimidation has allowed the alleged D-Gang to operate unchecked for decades, with connections that possibly extend into law enforcement and local governance, according to activists and whistleblowers.

Public Reaction

The assault led to an outpouring of support for the YouTubers. Hashtags like #DharmasthalaTruth, #JusticeForSoujanya, and #SupportIndependentMedia trended on regional social media platforms. Several citizen journalists and civil society activists condemned the attack as an attempt to silence investigative reporting.

Legal and Police Response

Despite the gravity of the incident, the response from local law enforcement has been widely criticized as inadequate and biased. While an FIR has reportedly been filed, no immediate arrests were made, and those involved in the attack have not yet been held accountable.

Locals and activists allege that the Dharmasthala police have consistently shown reluctance to take strong action, particularly in cases where powerful names or temple authorities are mentioned. This is not the first time their capability has been questioned—previous cases, including the Soujanya rape & murder case, have seen delays, inaction, and alleged tampering of evidence, all pointing to systemic weakness or potential collusion.

With growing distrust in the local police machinery, many are now demanding that all ongoing investigations be transferred to higher, impartial authorities like a High Court-monitored SIT or Supreme Court-monitored SIT. The lack of transparency and assertiveness from the local police continues to fuel public outrage and suspicion, reinforcing the belief that justice may not be served without external intervention.

Why This Matters

The attack is not just a local issue—it highlights a national concern: the increasing threats faced by independent journalists and YouTubers who dare to question those in power. In places like Dharmasthala, where spiritual authority meets social and political influence, such incidents reflect deeper systemic issues.

Conclusion

The Dharmasthala YouTuber attack is more than just another headline. It is a call to defend the fundamental rights of free speech, press freedom, and public accountability. If we fail to stand up for independent voices today, we risk losing the truth tomorrow.
