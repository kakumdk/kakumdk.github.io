How to identify a fake person?

On8th Jul 2023, 2025-06-04T13:18:48+05:30 ByKarthik Kumar D K | read
Listen Pause Resume Stop

Identifying a fake person can be challenging, but there are certain signs and behaviors that may indicate someone is being dishonest or not genuine. Here are some indicators to consider when trying to identify a fake person:

  1. Inconsistent Behavior: Pay attention to inconsistencies in their behavior, such as saying one thing and doing another, frequent changes in opinions, or exhibiting different personalities in different situations.
  2. Dishonesty: Look for signs of dishonesty, such as frequent lies, exaggerations, or a tendency to manipulate others. If you catch them in multiple lies or notice a pattern of deceit, it could be a red flag.
  3. Lack of Empathy: Fake people often lack genuine empathy and compassion. They may appear indifferent to the feelings and needs of others and their actions may primarily serve their own interests.
  4. Self-Centeredness: Pay attention to whether the person consistently puts their own needs and desires above others'. They may prioritize personal gain, seek attention and validation and show little regard for the well-being of others.
  5. Inauthenticity: Fake people may come across as superficial or insincere. They may engage in excessive flattery, use manipulative tactics, or pretend to be someone they're not in order to gain favor or advantage.
  6. Unreliability: Fake people often struggle to follow through on their commitments. They may frequently make promises they don't keep, cancel plans at the last minute without valid reasons, or consistently let others down.
  7. Gossiping and Backstabbing: If someone frequently engages in gossip, spreads rumors, or talks negatively about others behind their backs, it could be a sign of a fake person. They may try to create drama or manipulate others by spreading false information.
  8. Lack of Authentic Relationships: Fake people may have difficulty forming and maintaining genuine, deep relationships. They may have a large circle of acquaintances but lack close, long-lasting connections based on trust and mutual respect.
  9. Inconsistent Body Language: Pay attention to their body language, as it can provide cues about their authenticity. Fake people may exhibit incongruent non-verbal cues, such as forced smiles, avoidance of eye contact, or defensive postures.
  10. Trust Your Intuition: Trust your gut instincts and intuition when assessing someone's authenticity. If something feels off or you sense a lack of genuineness, it's worth paying attention to those feelings.

It's important to note that these signs are not definitive proof of someone being fake and it's essential to avoid making hasty judgments. People can display some of these behaviors at times or in certain situations. However, if you consistently notice these traits and behaviors in someone, it may be an indication that they are not genuine or trustworthy. It's important to set boundaries, maintain healthy skepticism and prioritize relationships with people who demonstrate authenticity, honesty and genuine care.
