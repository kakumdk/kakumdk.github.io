Bangalore to Kollur Solo Trip Plan

On13th Mar 2024, 2025-10-09T11:05:02+05:30 ByKarthik Kumar D K | read
Listen Pause Resume Stop

Solo trip from Bangalore to Kollur can be a serene and spiritually enriching experience, as Kollur is home to the famous Mookambika Temple.

Here's a suggested itinerary for your solo trip:

Bangalore to Kollur

  • Start your journey early in the morning to make the most of your day.
  • The distance between Bangalore and Kollur is approximately 400 kilometers and the drive takes around 8-9 hours.
  • Reach Kollur by late morning or early afternoon.
  • Check into your accommodation and freshen up.
  • Head to the Mookambika Temple, one of the Seven Muktisthala pilgrimage sites.
  • Seek blessings at the temple and explore its architectural and spiritual beauty.
  • Take a stroll around Kollur, exploring the local markets and enjoying the serene atmosphere.
  • If time permits, you can visit the nearby Kodachadri Hills for a nature trek.

Nature and Local Exploration

  • Explore nearby attractions like the Saraswati Mantap and Mookambika Wildlife Sanctuary.
  • Enjoy the natural beauty and serenity of the surroundings.
  • Spend some leisure time in Kollur, perhaps reading or reflecting.
  • Try local cuisine at a nearby eatery.
  • If you're interested in a short trek, you can consider visiting the Kodachadri Hills for a beautiful sunset view.
  • Return to Kollur for the night.

Departure

  • Visit the Mookambika Temple for morning rituals and spiritual reflection.
  • Spend some quiet time in the temple premises.
  • Check out from your accommodation.
  • Start your journey back to Bangalore.
  • Reach Bangalore by the evening, concluding your solo trip.

Please note:

  • Check the temple timings and any specific rituals happening during your visit.
  • Verify road conditions and take necessary precautions, especially if you're driving.
  • Respect the local customs and traditions during your visit to the temple.

This itinerary provides a general outline and you can adjust it based on your preferences and the time available. Always check for the latest travel advisories and conditions before embarking on your journey. Enjoy your solo trip to Kollur.
