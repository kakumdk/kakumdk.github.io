Indian style Spanish omelette, Egg + Potato + Onion

ByKarthik Kumar D Kon19th Feb 2023, 2023-02-19T11:00:00+05:30
Read Article
Pause
Resume
Stop

Egg with Potato plus Onion will make a delicious and healthy breakfast, You have have it as a Breakfast or you can have it as a Snack in the day, will also be a great starter in the barbecue. This combination of dish is called Spanish omelette, but lets make it in Indian Style.

Ingredients Needed?

3 Eggs, 4 medium sized Potato (chopped in small) & 4 medium sized Onion (chopped in small), 4-5 Green chilli (chopped in small), Chilli powder Oil & Salt for taste.

How to Cook?

1. Have a bowl on stove with low flame, Put 3-4 table spoons of Oil (as needed).. Put the chopped Onion, chopped Potato & chopped Chilli and Fry in the low flame.. Put some salt and continue to Fry for 6-8 min untill the stuff is medium fried..

2. Take the Fried stuff into another bowl and let it cool for a while, Meanwhile you prepare the other stuff

3. Take a bowl and break the Eggs & add Salt + Chilli powder and Mix it well, actually mix it for 2-3 min.. so that the mixture is thick..

4. Now mix the Fried stuff in to the Egg stuff and mix it well, Now you have the complete stuff ready which is ready for cooking..

5. Now have a tawa on the stove and Put 2-3 tea spoons of Oil and Pour the prepared stuff, Let it Fry in the low flame for 2-3 min.. Now Fry the other side of Omellete for 2-3 min.. Once its fried on both sides take it to out, its ready for serve.

Below is the detail of the time taken and number of people can have it.

  • Prep time: 30 minutes
  • Cook time: 15 minutes
  • Number of servings: 3 persons
