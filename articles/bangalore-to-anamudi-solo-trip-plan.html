Bangalore to Anamudi Solo Trip Plan

On4th Mar 2024, 2024-11-04T14:05:57+05:30 ByKarthik Kumar D K | read
Listen Pause Resume Stop

Anamudi, standing as the highest peak in the Western Ghats and South India, is a wonderful destination for nature lovers and trekkers. It's situated in the Eravikulam National Park near Munnar, Kerala.

Here's a suggested itinerary for your solo trip from Bangalore to Anamudi:

Bangalore to Munnar

  • Begin your journey early in the morning to make the most of your day.
  • The distance between Bangalore and Munnar is around 470 kilometers, and the drive takes approximately 9-10 hours.
  • Check into your accommodation in Munnar.
  • Spend the evening exploring the local market and enjoying the pleasant weather.

Munnar Exploration

  • Start your day by exploring the beautiful tea gardens of Munnar. You can visit places like the Tata Tea Museum for insights into tea processing.
  • Head to Eravikulam National Park, home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr. Enjoy the scenic beauty and wildlife.
  • Pothamedu Viewpoint offers a stunning view of the sunset over the hills. It's an ideal spot for nature lovers and photographers.

Trek to Anamudi

  • Begin your trek to Anamudi early in the morning. Make sure to get any required permits.
  • The trek offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscapes.
  • Reach the Summit of Anamudi and enjoy the panoramic views.
  • Start your descent in the afternoon.
  • Spend a relaxed evening after the trek, perhaps reflecting on your experience.

Departure

  • If you have energy left, you can visit nearby attractions like Mattupetty Dam or Kundala Lake.
  • Start your journey back to Bangalore in the afternoon.
  • Reach Bangalore by late night.

Please note:

  • Ensure you have all necessary permits for the trek to Anamudi.
  • Check the weather conditions before trekking.
  • Respect the local environment and follow responsible trekking practices.

This itinerary provides a general outline, and you can adjust it based on your preferences and the time available. Always check for the latest travel advisories and conditions before embarking on your journey. Enjoy your solo adventure to Anamudi.
